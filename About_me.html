About Me

Hi Everyone, My name is Chris.

I began working on this website project to continue on my learning journey in the technology space. I started my IT journey in 2018 when I decided to return to school to complete my Bachelor's Degree in Cybersecurity. Landing my first job outside of my automotive career with a Managed Service Provider, I was quickly introduced to the fast-paced world of Information Technology.

During my first year with the new MSP, I progressed quickly through the field taking on larger projects and more integral tasks with my team. This experience was a true blessing. As someone with no formal IT experience or schooling, I was able to prove my willingness to learn and grow within the field. Finalizing my time with my first company, I found an opportunity to move closer to home and continue on with another Managed Service Provider and take on a new set of challenges.

During this time I completed my Bachelor's Degree and chased my first Cybersecurity-related certification, the Security+. After successfully completing the certification, I became fully invested in the IT space. Ever-changing technology drives me to expand my knowledge in the field and stay at the forefront of the industry.
